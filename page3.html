Cloud Forest

The Cloud Forest is a mist-filled wonderland containing mountain flora that will take your breath away. Not just a feast for the eyes, you will also step away with a better understanding and appreciation for the environment and its conservation.

Flower Dome

Heaven is a place on Earth in the Flower Dome where flowers of all kinds come to life in the largest glass greenhouse in the world.

Supertree Grove

In the Grove, marvel at the majestic height of the man-made trees towering over everyone. The supertrees come alive at night with an exhilerating display of lights and music, culminating in a show not to be missed!

Floral Fantasy

Floral Fantasy is where dreams come true, with the aid of flowers, artistry and technology. Take a step into Floral Fantasy and experience the magic for yourself!

Heritage Gardens

The Heritage Gardens document Singapore's botanical culture and history with the replication of traditional Malay, Chinese and Indian gardens. Not only that, take a step through time and gaze at the peacefulness of colonial-era gardens as well!

Sun Pavilion

Singapore's sweltering heat is not perfect for sweating, it is also ideal for cultivating cacti! The aptly named Sun Pavilion contains over 1,000 cacti and desert flora, a display that is nothing short of amazing.
